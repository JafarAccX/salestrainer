Welcome to Sales Trainer

Follow this step-by-step guide to configure and manage your sales training program.

1

Upload Training Materials

Go to the Materials tab. Create knowledge base modules and upload your product PDFs, documentation, and sales playbooks. These documents power the AI's understanding of your product.

2

Configure AI Agent Personas

Navigate to AI Agents to create realistic prospect personas. Choose difficulty levels, set objection types, and customize the AI's behavior for different training scenarios.

3

Create Training Courses

In the Courses tab, create courses that link a knowledge base module with an AI agent. Set passing scores and publish when ready for your team.

4

Configure Global Settings

Visit Settings to set the active training module, default AI persona, and assessment timer. These apply to all reps using the sales rep dashboard.

5

Monitor & Review

Use Dashboard for performance overview, Transcripts to review call recordings, Evaluations for the leaderboard, and Token Usage to track costs.

6

Approve Certifications

When reps complete their evaluations, review and approve them in the Approvals tab. You can approve or send them back for another attempt.
