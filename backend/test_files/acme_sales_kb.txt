Acme Revenue Coach is a CRM-integrated sales enablement product for B2B teams.
It helps sales representatives prepare for calls, answer buyer objections, and practice mock discovery conversations.
The product connects to the company's CRM, reads approved product documents from the knowledge base, and gives reps grounded answers.
The main business value is faster onboarding, more consistent messaging, improved objection handling, and better sales manager visibility.
Common buyer objections include implementation time, trust in AI-generated answers, and price.
For implementation concerns, the recommended response is that the system starts with a focused pilot, uses existing CRM data, and limits answers to approved company documents.
For trust concerns, the recommended response is that the assistant answers only from uploaded knowledge-base documents and says when it does not have enough information.
For price concerns, the recommended response is to connect the cost to reduced ramp time, better rep productivity, and higher win-rate consistency.
